Magalam Hospital Telemedicine Proposal5
Department Focus

Why Pediatrics and Surgery are the right starting point

Pediatrics

  • High-value follow-up continuity for parents and children
  • Growth and immunization context matter across visits
  • Many reviews benefit from digital guidance before hospital revisit

Surgery

  • Pre-op clarification and post-op follow-up are ideal telemedicine use cases
  • Structured recovery review reduces unnecessary revisit load
  • Procedure-linked follow-up improves patient confidence

Board-level rationale: these two departments create clear patient value, measurable follow-up gains, and a fast proof of digital care impact.

Magalam Hospital Telemedicine Proposal6
Patient Journey

From request to continuity in one structured flow

  1. Patient creates profile and shares core health context.
  2. Patient requests appointment or teleconsult.
  3. Doctor or coordinator schedules the interaction.
  4. Consultation happens through a managed digital workflow.
  5. Doctor records notes, plan, prescription, and follow-up guidance.
  6. Patient retains continuity through records, notifications, and next-step visibility.

Better preparedness

Patients arrive with more context and fewer gaps.

Better continuity

Follow-up becomes operational, not accidental.

Better trust

Digital care feels like part of the hospital, not a disconnected tool.
