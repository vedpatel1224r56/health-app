# Medical Disclaimer (Draft v1)

SehatSaathi provides general informational guidance based on user-input symptoms.

- It is not a diagnostic system.
- It does not replace licensed medical professionals.
- It does not provide emergency services.

If symptoms are severe, worsening, or indicate emergency risk (for example chest pain, breathing difficulty, uncontrolled bleeding, loss of consciousness), seek immediate medical care.

Users should consult qualified clinicians before acting on any health decision.
