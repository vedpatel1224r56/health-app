# SehatSaathi Patient Onboarding (English)

## What this app does
- Gives first-line health guidance from your symptoms.
- Helps you decide if home care is enough or clinic visit is needed.
- Lets you share profile and recent guidance with a doctor using a short health pass code.

## How to use
1. Create account and complete profile.
2. Start symptom triage.
3. Read guidance and safety note.
4. If visiting clinic, generate health pass and show code to doctor.

## Safety note
This app is not a medical diagnosis. For emergencies, seek immediate medical care.

## Data and privacy
Your profile, triage, and consent are logged for continuity and safety. You can export or delete data.
